---
title: Tideline
date: 2026-06-20
field: poetry
---

The sea keeps no calendar
and still arrives on time,
laying its thin grey ledger
along the same torn line.

What it takes, it takes slowly —
a name, a step, a stone —
and what it gives back wholly
is the patience to go on.
